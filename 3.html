Cybersecurity engineer

Experts say that if you do not change the password periodically while using IT devices, it will be broken someday. Cybersecurity engineers are experts in crypto warfare against hackers. Recently, this job is so popular that people cannot find it. It is news that major companies such as credit rating agency Equifax and ticket seller Ticketmaster are reinforcing security systems significantly in preparation for the 5G era, and are greatly reinforcing manpower to operate new security systems. It is the “penetration testing” system that these companies are expanding their capabilities as targets. It refers to an ethical hacking system that diagnoses the possibility of infiltration from the outside using hacking tools and techniques.
